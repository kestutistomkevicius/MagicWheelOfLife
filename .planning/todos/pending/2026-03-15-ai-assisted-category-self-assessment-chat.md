## Problem

The blank "pick a number 1–10" scoring UX has friction — users stare at an empty slider with no guidance on what the score should reflect. A conversational approach would lower that barrier by replacing the raw input with a reflective dialogue.

## Solution

A conversational AI interface helps users assess their score for each life category through guided questions (e.g. "Tell me about your health habits this month" → AI suggests a score). Key behaviours:

- AI suggests as-is and/or to-be scores based on conversation outcome
- User confirms before any score is saved
- Stretch: AI can suggest renaming or reframing category labels based on what the user describes

Meaningful product differentiator. Capture as future feature — likely Phase 7+ after core scoring UX is solid.
